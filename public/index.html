Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on Creator Pay

💳 How do I pay for a resource?
All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. You can pay via credit/debit card, UPI, net banking, or digital wallets. You get instant access after payment.
📥 How do I access my purchased resource?
After purchase, go to your Dashboard. All purchased resources are listed there and available for download immediately.
💼 How do I sell my resources?
Sign up, go to Add Resources, upload your file, set a price, and publish. Your resource goes live after a quick review. No listing fees — ever.
💸 How does withdrawal work?
Request a withdrawal anytime from your Withdrawal page. A flat 5% platform fee is deducted. For example, withdraw ₹1,000 and receive ₹950 net — no other charges.
🔄 Can I get a refund?
Digital resources are generally non-refundable once accessed. However, we review refund requests for defective or misrepresented products on a case-by-case basis.
🛡️ Is my data safe?
Yes. Your account data is stored securely via Supabase with encryption. Payment data is handled exclusively by Razorpay — we never store your card or bank details.
